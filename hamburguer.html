Hamburguer

A juicy beef patty, grilled to perfection, nestled between two soft buns. Topped with your favorite condiments, such as ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise, and accompanied by fresh lettuce, tomato, and onion. A classic American dish that can be customized to your taste.

List of ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Hamburguer buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Prepare the patties: Combine ground beef with salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder. Form into patties.
  2. Grill the patties: Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook patties to desired doneness.
  3. Assemble the burgers: Toast the hamburger buns. Place a patty on each bottom bun. Top with cheese, if desired, and let it melt. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and your favorite condiments. Top with the top bun.
